About the Project

XOVER is an international center for graphic literature, built at the intersection of archival work, research, and publishing. The center is founded on a collection of over 2,500 comics, manga, and graphic novels, systematically organized according to global standards.

Our mission is to preserve and promote graphic heritage, connect local culture to a global context, and support researchers, authors, and readers around the world.

Publishing Projects

  • Visual Comments — annual zine based on social media folklore
  • Center Chronicle — annual review journal of acquisitions and events
  • Collection Bulletin — catalog of new arrivals with annotations

Recent Additions

  • Calugareni — Radu Theodoru, Romania, 1977
  • Barefoot Gen — complete set, first Russian edition
  • Blueberry — Jean Giraud (Mœbius), European comics legend
  • The Healing of Ilya Muromets — modern graphic epic
