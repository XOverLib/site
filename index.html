About the Center

The XOVER Center for Graphic Literature was founded in 2015 to study, preserve, and popularize comics and graphic novels.

Our mission is to make graphic literature accessible, build bridges between cultures, and support research.

Publishing Projects

  • 📘 Publication of rare comics and graphic novels in Russian
  • 📗 Educational programs and lectures on graphic literature
  • 📙 Research expeditions and archiving of comics heritage
